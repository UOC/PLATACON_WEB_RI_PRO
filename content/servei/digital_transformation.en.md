      **Description**


      We give you advice and tailor-made solutions to use ICT for your employees' digital skill building, implement efficient yet flexible teleworking models, and understand your customers' digital behaviour.


      **We therefore propose solutions:**


      * To improve the management of your organization's intellectual capital by considering more flexible and tailored work and professional development models.

      * To explore new customer relations solutions based on in-depth knowledge of their digital behaviour. 

      * To create and improve business opportunities on peer-to-peer digital platforms.


      **Our services**


      * **Digital marketing.** Analysis and tailor-made digital marketing solutions based on the knowledge of online consumers: impact of digital channels and technologies on organizations' value propositions; and strategic consulting to enhance immersive experiences and online consumer engagement. 




      * **Use of virtual environments.** Design and implementation of virtual environments, simulators and other technological tools to create, develop and assess personal and professional decision-making skills and competencies.




      * **Implementation and impact of teleworking.** Advice and studies on, and development of, programmes to allow teleworking and other forms of flexible working in order to improve staff management and well-being and help them achieve a better work-life balance.




      * **Business models on peer-to-peer digital platforms.** Advice regarding the operation of, and creation of value on, multilateral consumer-regulator platforms such as tourist accommodation platforms and open data ecosystems.




      * **Financial literacy and entrepreneurship.** Advice and training on financial literacy and entrepreneurship, online banking, financial and savings awareness, and entrepreneurship by women, among other matters. 




      * **Digital implementations**

      * * **Design and creation** of custom-made chatbots to handle user or customer queries.
        * Design and development of business simulators to acquire competencies.
        * Development of computer systems for decision-making based on synthetic models and/or real data.
        * **Immersive experiences**. Design and creation of immersive digital experiences using virtual reality, augmented reality and mixed reality, with and without geolocation.
        * Local and global positioning systems. Tailor-made developments using radio and satellite communication technologies that make it possible to connect assets and equipment both locally and globally.
        * Natural language processing and automatic translation assistance technologies.
        * User privacy and content protection. Watermarking and encryption systems for the secure storage and transmission of multimedia information and data.
        * **Cybersecurity.** Prevention, detection, investigation and creation of tools in response to incidents or threats on electronic communication networks and public information systems.

      **Our value**


      1. The UOC, **which became the first fully online** university in the world in 1995, is an organization with extensive experience and expertise in ICT and digitization, having carried out specialist research in the interaction between technology and human and social sciences. 



      2. The **interdisciplinary teams** that have existed at the UOC ever since it was created have encouraged the digital transformation of work and consumption and assessed its impact. This has enabled it to approach digital transformation both from the point of view of technological tools and solutions and based on the rigorous analysis of its design, implementation and assessment for both users and organizations.



      3. [The Internet Interdisciplinary Institute](https://research.uoc.edu/portal/en/in3/index.html) (IN3), which **has existed at the UOC** since the year 2000, aims to develop digital-age technological solutions and study the internet and the effects of the interaction between digital technologies and human activity. 




      **Who is it aimed at?**


      * All kinds of organizations that see **teleworking or other less traditional** working options as a flexibility and talent retention strategy.

      * **Public administrations** and large corporations with the ability to deploy tailor-made digital training programmes.

      * **Marketing and communication** teams and companies.

      * Human resources departments and other professional development units.
