Centres

Internet Interdisciplinary Institute (IN3) External link

A research centre specializing in the study of the internet and the effects of the interaction between digital technologies and human activity.

eLearn Center External link

Facilitating applied research in e-learning, fostering innovation in this field, and conveying the value of e-learning as essential to the UOC's identity, both inside and outside the university.

eHealth Center External link

The eHealth Center is an academic centre open to the world whose goal is to educate and empower professionals and ordinary citizens, through the use of technologies, to lead the paradigm shift in health.
